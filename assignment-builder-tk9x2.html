Dependencies & risks
⚠️
KSB coverage is scraper-dependent
Scraper holds 385 standards but 106 have empty KSBs — L3 CSS (ST0071) was one, now pulled manually from Skills England. Backfill the rest before mapping at scale.
🔖
Official codes vs AC re-coding
The gold doc uses AC's scheme (Kn-CSEA1, Sk-SI1). Anchor to official IfATE codes for trust; treat AC's grouping as a presentation layer.
📋
Copy risk on the exemplar
Hold the tension: relatable enough to teach, distinct enough to be uncopyable.
🔐
Architecture / data
Needs an LLM (Claude API). Internal-first keeps hosting/GDPR light, but real learner scenarios are personal data — handle with care even in-house.
