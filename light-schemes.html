Light-mode schemes

Five directions for the calculator's light mode. Every one sits on a pure white page — no silver, no gradient, no glare. What differs is the palette and, importantly, how each section is designated (background tint vs. border vs. top bar vs. left rail vs. label chip).

Each demo shows the same four section types — SETUP · RESULTS · WATCH · metric tiles — so you can compare how the design tells them apart. Pick a number (and mix-and-match a designation method with a palette if you like).
